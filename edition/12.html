OIS 2020/21

349 teams from 95 schools participated in this edition of the OIS, scoring a total of 214253 points on 42 tasks. The top 3 teams at the finals were:

  1. Btwice from Volta, Milano
  2. Curieteam21 from LS M. Curie, Giulianova
  3. Galilegends from Galileo Galilei, Trento

Highlights

  1. Una Lacrima Sul Visto is the most difficult task of this edition.
  2. ITI Planck, Villorba is the institute with the most points (9394).
  3. Lombardia is the region with the most points (36258).
  4. Btwice is the team with the best awards.
