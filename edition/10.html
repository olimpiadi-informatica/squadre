OIS 2018/19

336 teams from 97 schools participated in this edition of the OIS, scoring a total of 197083 points on 40 tasks. The top 3 teams at the finals were:

  1. LSFoligno2 from Liceo G. Marconi, Foligno
  2. ARACHIDI VELOCI from I.I.S. F. Alberghetti, Imola
  3. Sushi Squad from ITST Kennedy, Pordenone

Highlights

  1. Railway Schedule is the most difficult task of this edition.
  2. Galileo Galilei, Trento is the institute with the most points (12243).
  3. Lombardia is the region with the most points (33223).
  4. LSFoligno2 is the team with the best awards.
