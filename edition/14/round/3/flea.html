Statement

A flea lives on the number line, his home is at point 0. He woke up today and decided to jump around a bit. With each jump, he can move one unit to the left or one unit to the right of his current position. After making N jumps, he got tired and homesick, and now he wants to go back to point 0 with the minimal number of jumps. How many jumps does he have to make to get back to his original position?
