OIS 2019/20

505 teams from 132 schools participated in this edition of the OIS, scoring a total of 373787 points on 42 tasks. The top 3 teams at the finals were:

  1. Sushi Squad from ITST Kennedy, Pordenone
  2. Arachidi Veloci from I.I.S. F. Alberghetti, Imola
  3. BToiS from Volta, Milano

Highlights

  1. Pulsating Radars is the most difficult task of this edition.
  2. Galileo Galilei, Trento is the institute with the most points (12414).
  3. Emilia-Romagna is the region with the most points (55953).
  4. Sushi Squad is the team with the best awards.
