OIS 2015/16

210 teams from 61 schools participated in this edition of the OIS, scoring a total of 100635 points on 35 tasks. The top 3 teams at the finals were:

  1. ACD++ from Enrico Fermi, Cantù
  2. Blaise 1 from Pascal - Comandini, Cesena
  3. Moro1 from Aldo Moro, Reggio Nell'Emilia

Highlights

  1. Codici interessanti is the most difficult task of this edition.
  2. Pascal - Comandini, Cesena is the institute with the most points (6105).
  3. Emilia-Romagna is the region with the most points (27725).
  4. Blaise 1 is the team with the best awards.
