OIS 2024/25

791 teams from 196 schools participated in this edition of the OIS, scoring a total of 290878 points on 44 tasks. The top 3 teams at the finals were:

  1. .cppernico from Liceo Scientifico N. Copernico, Udine
  2. PGS from LS G.D. Cassini, Genova
  3. theBuggers from Giovanni Falcone, Asola

Highlights

  1. And the Rain Continues to Come Down is the most difficult task of this edition.
  2. Pascal - Comandini, Cesena is the institute with the most points (7085).
  3. Lombardia is the region with the most points (55882).
