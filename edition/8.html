OIS 2016/17

356 teams from 96 schools participated in this edition of the OIS, scoring a total of 116608 points on 35 tasks. The top 3 teams at the finals were:

  1. Marconi2016 from Marconi - Mangano, Catania
  2. Galilei 02 from Galileo Galilei, Trento
  3. Sintax Error from T. Buzzi, Prato

Highlights

  1. Mosaic Composition is the most difficult task of this edition.
  2. Galileo Galilei, Trento is the institute with the most points (5880).
  3. Lombardia is the region with the most points (20455).
  4. Arachidi Veloci is the team with the best awards.
