Statement

Whenever the room alarm signals that lunch is ready, the whole staff rushes for the exit of the room, which is located by workstation 0, while orderly following the evacuation plan. The evacuation plan of the room has a tree shape, so that anyone in workstation i has only one option for going towards the exit, which is workstation E_i (we say that E_0 = -1 to represent that workstation 0 is connected to the exit). However, multiple workstations i, j may compete for the same E_i = E_j, forcing the staff members to use their arrogance to move forward.
