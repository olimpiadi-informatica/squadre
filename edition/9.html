OIS 2017/18

418 teams from 104 schools participated in this edition of the OIS, scoring a total of 165851 points on 35 tasks. The top 3 teams at the finals were:

  1. Magistrini from Magistri Cumacini, Como
  2. Arachidi Veloci from I.I.S. F. Alberghetti, Imola
  3. Kennedy from ITST Kennedy, Pordenone

Highlights

  1. Project groups is the most difficult task of this edition.
  2. Galileo Galilei, Trento is the institute with the most points (8545).
  3. Lombardia is the region with the most points (32805).
  4. Magistrini is the team with the best awards.
